About IBBUL

Ibrahim Badamasi Babangida University, Lapai (IBBUL) is Niger State's premier state-owned university, founded to expand access to high-level education and promote intensive research.

Our Vision

To be a World-Class Centre of excellence driven by people, partnership, and technology.

Our Mission

Committed to producing world-class graduates for the pursuit of all-round excellence through quality research, teaching, and community service.

Our Motto

"Learning for Service"

Serene Main Campus & Modern Facilities

Located in Lapai, the university provides a serene rural academic environment heavily tailored towards cutting-edge technology, equipped with an E-Learning center and a digital repository.
